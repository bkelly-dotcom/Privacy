Privacy Policy

Geomantia does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind. Your readings stay on your device and belong only to you.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Geomantia ("the App"), developed by Lots o' Dots, handles your information. We are committed to your privacy. This policy is straightforward because there is very little to say — Geomantia is designed from the ground up to collect no data whatsoever.

Information We Do Not Collect

Geomantia does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Location data of any kind
  • Usage data, analytics, or behavioral tracking
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to us
  • Reading history or divination content
  • Payment information (all purchases are processed entirely by Apple)
  • Cookies or any tracking technologies
How Your Data Is Stored

Any data generated by your use of Geomantia — including your reading history and any preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

In-App Purchases

Geomantia offers optional in-app purchases including casting method unlocks and skin packs. All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Lots o' Dots does not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles purchase data.

Third-Party Services

Geomantia does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar tools.

The only third-party interaction is with Apple's App Store for the purpose of processing in-app purchases, which is governed entirely by Apple's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Geomantia does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because the App collects no data at all, it presents no privacy risk to users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy — for example, if a future version of the App introduces new features — we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the App Store update notes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Any future data collection would only occur with your explicit knowledge and, where required by law, your consent.
